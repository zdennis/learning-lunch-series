How?

  • NFA / backtracking implementation.
  • Incremental development (one feature at a time)
  • Test drive with RSpec (focus on behavior, not implementation)
  • Implement in Ruby

Expectations

  • Each person implements their own regex engine
  • 2 - 3 features a week
  • Meet twice a week as a group for about an hour
    • Review progress and implementation together, Q&A
    • Identify and discuss next regex feature to implement
  • Everyone has their own Git repository (your public or MH private)

Scheduling

  • Let's pick the two day/times to meet as a group
  • Outside of group meet your schedule is your schedule
  • If pace proves too aggressive we can always adjust the schedule
